HOW TO PLAY

Pull the face apart one trail at a time.

  1. Tap an arrow trail whose arrowhead has a clear lane to the edge.
  2. The arrowhead exits first and the connected trail follows.
  3. A blocked trail stays in place and highlights the first visible blocker.
  4. Use Hint or Open arrow trails when you need a clear move.
