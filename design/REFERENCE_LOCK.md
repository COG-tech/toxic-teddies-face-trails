# Gameplay Visual Reference Lock

Do not claim the gameplay screen is finished until it visually matches the approved portrait arrow-puzzle reference.

Locked requirements:

- full-height portrait board occupying most of the viewport;
- connected line-arrow pieces, not isolated unit-arrow glyphs;
- cream parchment background;
- three blue life drops below the header;
- centered `Level X` title;
- minimal interface with no gameplay side panel;
- Toxic Teddy artwork used only as a subtle board backdrop;
- puzzle pieces should visually form a Teddy face/body composition;
- blocked taps cost a life;
- long-press previews a piece trajectory;
- pieces slide off the board when their lane is clear.
