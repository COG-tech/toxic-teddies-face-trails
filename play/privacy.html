TOXIC TEDDIES

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

Toxic Teddies: Arrow Escape is designed to work with game code, levels, and artwork bundled inside the installed app. The current release does not require an account and does not contain advertising, purchases, or third-party network analytics.

Information the app stores

The app stores puzzle progress and accessibility settings locally on the device. This includes completed levels, removed path identifiers for an unfinished puzzle, and settings such as reduced motion, high contrast, and touch assistance.

Optional research logging

A research-build option may record approved interaction events locally, such as level starts, successful moves, blocked moves, missed taps, hints, restarts, saves, restores, and completions. Research logging is disabled by default. It does not record raw touch coordinates, continuous finger movement, names, email addresses, advertising identifiers, or account identifiers.

Research data remains on the device unless the person using the app deliberately chooses to export it from Settings. Export uses the device's normal share or copy controls.

Data collection and sharing

The current native app does not transmit personal data to COG-tech and does not sell or share personal data with advertisers or data brokers. Native sharing is user-initiated and is handled by the operating system and the destination selected by the user.

Permissions

The Android package includes internet capability for Capacitor WebView compatibility and optional user-initiated external links. Launch gameplay is bundled locally and does not require a network connection. The app does not request access to location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, health data, or advertising identifiers.

Children

The app contains stylized gross-out cartoon imagery. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults because the current release has no account system or server-side data collection.

Browser demo

The optional GitHub Pages browser demo is hosted by GitHub. GitHub may process standard web request information under its own policies. This hosting activity is separate from the installed native app's local gameplay and local research logging.

Changes

This policy will be updated before any release that adds accounts, advertising, purchases, cloud saves, remote analytics, or other material data practices.

Contact and support

Questions can be submitted through the Toxic Teddies support tracker.
