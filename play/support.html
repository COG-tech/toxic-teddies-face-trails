TOXIC TEDDIES

Support

Toxic Teddies: Arrow Escape is an offline-first mobile puzzle. Toxic Toby currently has five playable expressions; the other Founding Teddies are intentionally marked Coming soon.

Quick fixes

  1. Install the latest app update.
  2. Close and reopen the app. Unfinished progress should restore automatically.
  3. Open Settings and confirm whether Reduced motion, High contrast, or Touch assistance is enabled.
  4. Use Hint or Open arrow trails to identify a path whose arrowhead has a clear lane to the edge.
  5. Reinstall only after recording the app version shown in Settings, because uninstalling may remove local progress.

Report a problem

Open a support issue and include:

  • iPhone or Android device model;
  • operating-system version;
  • app version and build number from Settings;
  • Toxic Toby expression and level;
  • steps that reproduce the problem;
  • whether the device was offline.

Do not include passwords, signing keys, private account information, or other sensitive data.
